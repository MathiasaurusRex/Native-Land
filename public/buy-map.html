Buy Map?

I have not sold any maps from this site, nor do I currently have any for sale. I have some reservations about selling maps as a non-indigenous person.

However, I have gotten significant interest from people when it comes to a physical version of the map. This is fairly hard since it is constantly changing and is admittedly very difficult to do in the first place, for many reasons outlined elsewhere on this site.

That being said, if you are interested in having a printed version of the map, please send me an email at tempranova@gmail.com. If I get enough emails I will put something together that will help support the site -- not to turn a profit.

Thanks!
